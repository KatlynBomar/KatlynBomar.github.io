A Journey in Science and Art

Originally from Sandusky, Ohio, Katlyn grew up surrounded by nature. Ever since she was little, she has explored forests and the lake. This sparked a love for the environment and all of its inhabitants and eventually human anatomy. When a love for art blossomed in highschool, Katlyn found a way to pursue both of her interests, science and art.

Follow along as she navigates college to pursue her dream of teaching others about science through her art!
